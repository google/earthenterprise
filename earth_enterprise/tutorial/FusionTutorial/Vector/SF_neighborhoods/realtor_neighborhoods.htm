What does this data set describe?

Title: Neighborhoods - SF Assoc. of Realtors
Abstract:
San Francisco Neighborhoods as designated by the San Francisco Association of Realtors (SFAR). The data set was digitized from paper maps provided by Public Affairs personnel. DPW/ Public Affairs drew boundaries and assigned designations to 88 distinct neighborhoods. Note: Chinatown is not designated.
