What is Google Earth Enterprise Client (EC)?

Google Earth Enterprise Client (Google Earth EC) is part of Google Earth Enterprise, which lets organizations build, host, and view private globes created from their own geospatial data. Government agencies and businesses can run Google Earth Enterprise in their own data centers, keeping their sensitive data behind the firewall with access restricted to authorized employees.

Google Earth EC lets you connect directly to your organization’s private globes. To host the globes, the database servers need to run the Google Earth Enterprise Server software. Organizations can host globes on internal servers only or can layer their data on top of Google’s global database of satellite imagery and mapping information.
