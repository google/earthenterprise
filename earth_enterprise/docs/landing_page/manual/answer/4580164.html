Welcome to Portable 5.0

If you purchased a Google Earth Enterprise license and received an email from Google with the new authentication credentials that you need to download your Portable software, you’re ready to set up Portable and start viewing offline globes and maps! Just take a few moments before you get started to review the following information. 

Using this Help Center

The Google Earth Enterprise Help Center provides information about setting up and using the desktop version of Google Earth Enterprise Portable (GEE Portable). Make sure to check the Help Center before contacting the Support team.

Downloading Portable

If you purchased Portable directly from Google, you should've received an email that contains authentication credentials for the Google Enterprise Support Portal. If you are listed as the technical contact for your order and haven’t received your credentials within two business days of your purchase, submit a request for credentials.

To access and download your software:

  1. Log in to the Google Enterprise Support Portal with the authentication credentials you received by email.
  2. Click the Resources tab in the upper left corner.
  3. Find your purchased product installers in the list. The Resource Type is Files.
