Before you configure Google Earth Enterprise Fusion

Before you use many of the Google Earth Enterprise Fusion configuration tools, you must stop the system manager and then start it after you change the configuration. To stop/start the system manager, enter:

/etc/init.d/gefusion [stop | start | restart]
